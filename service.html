Loose Cargo

For customers looking to ship items that do not fill up one container, Loose Cargo Shipments are the way to go. From one box all the way to more than one boxes, we can ship your goods to wherever you need them, depending on the customers choice of convenience or cost. Loose cargo shipments are charged by weight rate (kg) or volume (cubic meter), whichever is higher.

Full Container

If you require shipping for one whole container, we are able to provide you hassle-free service of either pier to pier, door to door, door to pier - whichever more of service will be the most cost-effective and convenient for you. We ship with all the major shipping companies, we are able to offer you better rates and better services.

Rolling Cargo (Cars, Truck & Equipment)

In as much as it is now possible to drive cars and trucks mostly anywhere in the country today, the wear and tear would be unacceptable. We can assist you in loading Rolling Cargo so as to make sure that your vehicles can be delivered to your clients in the pristine condition that they expect.
